This system manages the performance assessment of the individual employees and its alignment to the agency's organizational vision, mission and strategic goals. It records the targets and accomplishments of the agency, division and employees, and generates the Office Performance Commitment and Review (OPCR), Division Performance Commitment and Review (DPCR), and Individual Performance Commitment and Review (IPCR) reports.
